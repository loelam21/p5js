Crabtonaut

Background Story:

You were abandoned by your fellow crab astronauts for being too soft shelled. Now, you must face the consequences and survive on your own.


How To Play:

- You are the soft shelled astronaut.

- Hit spacebar to float up.

- "a" to move left. "d" to move right.

- Avoid the asteroids.

- Gain points when asteroids go offscreen.

- "r" to restart.

- Have fun!



Hit ENTER to start.
